Some of our software Bespoke Application examples

Sales enablement platform

This smart integrated platform allows our client's sales staff to manage, share and track a large number of documents with their customers and create unique presentations on the go. It is an integrated solution with various third party connections like Microsoft outlook, Google Drive, Single Sign-on (SSO) etc.

Remote patient management

AI-driven remote patient monitoring solution for patients with chronic conditions empowers them to manage their condition from home with regular communication with clinicians. It also utilises an intelligent custom analytics platform predicting serious episodes and alerting patients and clinicians of the same. The platform uses Azure(PaaS) infrastructure to ensure scalability and high availability. The platform is CE marked and as with all of our software builds it is also OWASP compliant with penetration testing carried out by CREST certified external security team.

Bespoke CRM solution

We developed a smart bespoke CRM solution customised to our client's requirements and business processes. The solution automated all the marketing communications and provided role-based access with robust integrations to various third-party services such as Sendgrid, Twilio etc. It is a complete solution with mobile apps and website for end-users, which are managed via the backend CRM solution as well.

Software Development Life Cycle

We employ best practices and development methodologies for building effective software development processes and solutions in a structured and systematic way.

1

Designing

Once the requirement analysis phase is completed, the next step is to define and document the software needs.

2

Planning

We collect all the relevant information from the customer to develop custom software development solutions as per their expectations

3

Building

Developers start to build the system by writing code using the chosen programming language, techniques, and methodologies.

4

Testing

Evaluating the quality of software with the aim of finding and fixing defects.

5

Deployment

The final software is released and checked for deployment issues.

6

Maintenance

According to the service level agreement, we ensure that needs continue to be met and that the system continues to perform as per the specification agreed in the first phase.
