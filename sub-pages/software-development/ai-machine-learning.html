Our process for successful AI implementation

We strongly believe a road to successful AI and ML adoption is a journey and the following steps ensure we implement high-quality AI solutions.

1

Define use case

Our project starts by clearly defining the problem or challenge we are helping the organisation to solve by implementing AI

2

Verify data availability

Once the use case is clearly defined, we ensure the processes and systems in place are capable of capturing and tracking the data needed to perform the required analysis.

3

Data Exploration

We undertake a data exploration exercise to validate data assumptions and understanding ensuring data is telling the right story based on the subject matter expertise.

4

Define model building

We run tests to determine which variables or features are more significant to validate the hypothesis and improve execution. We ensure domain and business experts are involved to provide continuous feedback to create a successful ML model.

5

Define model validation

The definition of performance measures assists in the evaluation, comparison and analysis of results from multiple algorithms and help to further refine specific models

6

Automation and deployment

Once the model has been built and validated, it is then rolled out into production. Beginning with a limited rollout of a few weeks or months, upon which business users provide continuous feedback on the model behaviour and outcome, it can then be rolled out to the wider audience.

7

Evaluate and Refine

Once a model has been published and deployed for use, it is continuously monitored as, by understanding its validity, an organisation will be able to update the model as required.
